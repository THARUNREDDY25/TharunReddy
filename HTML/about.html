About Me

I’m Tharun, a video editor focused on creating clean, modern and watchable edits for social media, YouTube and events. I enjoy turning raw, messy footage into clear stories that hold attention.

My work ranges from short Instagram reels and YouTube videos to wedding highlights and promo content. I pay a lot of attention to pacing, music, color and sound so the final video feels polished and intentional.

I’m comfortable working with creators, small businesses and individuals, and I can adapt to your preferred style or help you find one that fits you.

Tools I work with:
Adobe Premiere Pro After Effects Photoshop CapCut / Mobile edits
Types of projects:
Reels & Shorts YouTube videos Wedding highlights Promos & ads Event recaps
Quick Facts

Turnaround: 1–5 days for short-form content (depends on project).

Deliverables: Ready-to-upload videos in 9:16, 16:9, 1:1 or custom sizes.

Revisions: Basic revisions included so we can polish the final cut.

Languages: English, Telugu, Hindi (for communication and subtitles if needed).
